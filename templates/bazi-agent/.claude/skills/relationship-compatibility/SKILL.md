# Relationship Compatibility Analysis

This skill analyzes BaZi compatibility between two people for romantic relationships, commonly known as 合婚 (Hé Hūn). It examines how their charts interact, identifies harmonious and challenging aspects, and provides practical relationship advice.

## When to Use This Skill

- **Pre-marriage consultation**: Traditional compatibility check
- **Relationship counseling**: Understanding couple dynamics
- **Dating advice**: Assessing new relationship potential
- **Conflict resolution**: Understanding underlying tensions
- **Timing marriage**: Finding auspicious periods for wedding

## What This Skill Does

1. **Chart Comparison**: Analyzes both BaZi charts side-by-side
2. **Element Harmony**: Examines if elements support or conflict
3. **Day Master Interaction**: How core personalities mesh
4. **Life Path Alignment**: Whether life directions are compatible
5. **Challenge Identification**: Potential areas of friction
6. **Strength Assessment**: Overall compatibility score and outlook
7. **Practical Advice**: Relationship strategies for harmony

## Compatibility Factors

### 1. Day Master Harmony (日主相合)

**Highly Compatible Pairs**:
- **Generating Relationships**: One element nourishes the other
  - Water Day Master + Wood Day Master (Water generates Wood)
  - Wood Day Master + Fire Day Master
  - Fire Day Master + Earth Day Master
  - Earth Day Master + Metal Day Master
  - Metal Day Master + Water Day Master

- **Same Element**: Mutual understanding
  - Wood + Wood: Grow together
  - Fire + Fire: Passionate bond
  - Earth + Earth: Stable foundation
  - Metal + Metal: Principled partnership
  - Water + Water: Deep connection

**Challenging Pairs**:
- **Controlling Relationships**: One controls/restrains the other
  - Water Day Master + Fire Day Master (Water controls Fire)
  - Fire Day Master + Metal Day Master
  - Metal Day Master + Wood Day Master
  - Wood Day Master + Earth Day Master
  - Earth Day Master + Water Day Master

*Note*: Controlling relationships aren't necessarily bad - they can create growth through challenge, but require more awareness and effort.

### 2. Yin-Yang Balance (阴阳配合)

**Ideal**: One Yang, one Yin (e.g., 甲 + 乙, 丙 + 丁)
- Creates complementary dynamic
- Balance between active and receptive
- Natural give-and-take

**Same Polarity**: Both Yang or both Yin
- Can work but may lack balance
- Yang + Yang: High energy but potential conflict
- Yin + Yin: Harmonious but may lack initiative

### 3. Elemental Balance (五行互补)

**Complementary**: Partner provides missing elements
- Person A lacks Water → Person B strong in Water
- Creates mutual support and completion

**Competing**: Both have same excesses/deficiencies
- Both lack Earth → relationship lacks stability
- Both excessive Fire → burnout, conflicts

### 4. Zodiac Compatibility (生肖相合)

**Six Harmonies (六合)**:
- Rat + Ox, Tiger + Pig, Rabbit + Dog
- Dragon + Rooster, Snake + Monkey, Horse + Goat

**Three Harmonies (三合)**:
- Rat-Dragon-Monkey, Ox-Snake-Rooster
- Tiger-Horse-Dog, Rabbit-Goat-Pig

**Six Clashes (六冲)** - Challenging:
- Rat ↔ Horse, Ox ↔ Goat, Tiger ↔ Monkey
- Rabbit ↔ Rooster, Dragon ↔ Dog, Snake ↔ Pig

**Six Harms (六害)** - Friction:
- Rat ↔ Goat, Ox ↔ Horse, Tiger ↔ Snake
- Rabbit ↔ Dragon, Monkey ↔ Pig, Rooster ↔ Dog

### 5. Luck Cycle Alignment (大运流年)

- Are they in compatible or conflicting cycles?
- Do they face challenges at the same time (mutual support)?
- Are they in different life phases (growth vs. stability)?
