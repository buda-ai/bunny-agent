# Career Path Advisor

This skill provides personalized career guidance based on BaZi analysis, matching natural talents (elemental strengths) with suitable professions, work environments, and career strategies.

**💼 For Modern Professionals**: This is NOT fortune-telling - it's a **strategic career assessment tool** that:
- Maps your natural strengths to market opportunities
- Identifies work environments where you'll thrive
- Highlights optimal timing for career transitions
- Provides data-driven recommendations for skill development

**Think of it as**: Personality assessment (MBTI/StrengthsFinder) + Market analysis + Timing strategy

> "八字不决定职业，但揭示你的天赋优势。选择权在你手中。"  
> *BaZi doesn't determine your career - it reveals your natural advantages. The choice is yours.*

## When to Use This Skill

- **Career exploration**: Choosing a career path
- **Career transition**: Changing fields or industries
- **Job search**: Identifying suitable roles and companies
- **Business planning**: Starting or growing a venture
- **Professional development**: Skill-building priorities
- **Work-life balance**: Finding sustainable career approach

## What This Skill Does

1. **Element-Career Mapping**: Matches strong elements to careers
2. **Day Master Career Traits**: Identifies natural work style and strengths
3. **Work Environment Fit**: Recommends ideal organizational cultures
4. **Career Timing**: Identifies favorable periods for transitions
5. **Skill Development**: Suggests areas for growth
6. **Success Strategies**: Provides tactics based on chart
7. **Avoid Pitfalls**: Highlights mismatched careers and why

## Analysis Framework

### Step 1: Identify Core Strengths

From BaZi chart:
- **Day Master**: Core work style and approach
- **Strongest Element**: Primary talent area
- **Element Balance**: Complementary skills available

### Step 2: Match to Career Categories

Map strengths to career fields:
- Primary careers (strong element + Day Master match)
- Secondary careers (supporting elements)
- Growth careers (develop weaker elements)

### Step 3: Consider Current Cycle

Adjust for timing:
- Favorable cycle: Bold moves, leadership
- Challenging cycle: Skill-building, support roles
- Transition period: Preparation, not action

### Step 4: Assess Work Environment Fit

Organizational culture matters:
- Startup vs. corporate
- Structured vs. flexible
- Solo vs. team-oriented
- Stable vs. dynamic

### Step 5: Provide Strategic Guidance

Actionable advice:
- Immediate next steps
- Short-term tactics (1-2 years)
- Long-term strategy (5-10 years)
- Skills to develop
- Roles to pursue/avoid
