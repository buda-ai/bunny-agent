# Zodiac Profile Manager

This skill creates, maintains, and updates detailed zodiac sign profiles that serve as reference material for horoscope writing, compatibility analysis, and user education. Each profile is a comprehensive guide to a zodiac sign's personality, traits, and astrological characteristics.

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating reference profiles for all 12 zodiac signs
- Updating sign profiles with seasonal or annual themes
- Generating educational content about zodiac signs
- Creating "About Your Sign" pages for the platform
- Producing comprehensive sign guides
- Maintaining consistent sign characterizations

## What This Skill Does

1. **Profile Creation**: Generates complete zodiac sign profiles
2. **Trait Documentation**: Details personality characteristics
3. **Compatibility Overview**: Summarizes relationship dynamics
4. **Astrological Data**: Compiles dates, elements, planets, etc.
5. **Career & Life Guidance**: Provides sign-specific advice
6. **Strengths & Challenges**: Balances positive and growth areas
7. **Famous Examples**: Lists notable people of each sign

## Output Format

Each zodiac profile includes:

### Basic Information
- Sign name and symbol
- Date range
- Element (Fire, Earth, Air, Water)
- Modality (Cardinal, Fixed, Mutable)
- Ruling planet(s)
- Lucky numbers, colors, days
- Compatible signs
- Opposite sign

### Personality Overview
- Core traits (300-400 words)
- Positive qualities
- Challenges and growth areas
- Emotional nature
- Behavioral patterns
- Life philosophy

### Detailed Characteristics
- In love and relationships
- In career and money
- In friendship and social life
- As a family member
- In health and wellness

### Compatibility Summary
- Most compatible signs
- Challenging pairings
- Friendship compatibility
- Professional compatibility

### Life Guidance
- Ideal careers
- Best life paths
- Relationship advice
- Personal growth tips
- Success strategies
