# Share Content Generator

This skill transforms horoscope content into optimized, shareable formats for social media platforms. Creates engaging posts, quote cards, and snippets designed to maximize virality and user engagement.

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating social media posts from horoscopes
- Generating shareable quote graphics
- Optimizing content for different platforms
- Creating viral horoscope snippets
- Designing engagement-driving posts
- Batch-creating social content

## What This Skill Does

1. **Platform Optimization**: Formats content for Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, TikTok
2. **Quote Extraction**: Pulls the most shareable insights
3. **Hashtag Generation**: Creates relevant, trending hashtags
4. **Visual Direction**: Suggests imagery and design elements
5. **Engagement Hooks**: Crafts compelling CTAs and questions
6. **Emoji Integration**: Strategically uses emojis for visibility
7. **Batch Creation**: Generates multiple post variations

## Platform Specifications

### 📸 Instagram
- **Format**: Feed post, Story, Reel, Carousel
- **Character Limit**: 2,200 (optimal: 125-150 for caption)
- **Hashtags**: 20-30 (optimal: 11-15)
- **Style**: Visual-first, lifestyle-oriented
- **CTA**: "Save this post", "Share with a friend", "DM us"

### 🐦 Twitter/X
- **Format**: Single tweet, Thread
- **Character Limit**: 280 per tweet
- **Hashtags**: 1-2 (not excessive)
- **Style**: Punchy, conversational
- **CTA**: "Retweet if you agree", "Reply with your sign"

### 📘 Facebook
- **Format**: Standard post, Story
- **Character Limit**: Unlimited (optimal: 40-80 characters for engagement)
- **Hashtags**: 1-3 (less important)
- **Style**: Community-building, shareable
- **CTA**: "Tag a [zodiac sign] friend", "Share if this resonates"

### 🎵 TikTok
- **Format**: Video caption
- **Character Limit**: 2,200 (optimal: 150)
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 trending
- **Style**: Trendy, authentic, relatable
- **CTA**: "Duet this", "Stitch your reaction", "Follow for daily horoscopes"

## Writing Guidelines

### Platform-Specific Tone

#### Instagram
- **Voice**: Aspirational, aesthetic, lifestyle
- **Length**: Short captions (125-150 chars) perform best
- **Emojis**: Generous use, visually appealing
- **Focus**: Visual storytelling, aesthetics

#### Twitter/X
- **Voice**: Witty, conversational, timely
- **Length**: Short and punchy, thread for depth
- **Emojis**: Strategic, not excessive
- **Focus**: Conversation starters, shareability

#### Facebook
- **Voice**: Warm, community-oriented, detailed
- **Length**: Medium-form (200-300 words)
- **Emojis**: Moderate use
- **Focus**: Connection, tagging, sharing

#### TikTok
- **Voice**: Authentic, trendy, relatable
- **Length**: Brief captions with hooks
- **Emojis**: Trending, expressive
- **Focus**: Video-friendly, trend-aligned

### Engagement Best Practices

1. **Lead with Value**: Start with the benefit
2. **Use Active Voice**: Direct, energetic language
3. **Create FOMO**: Time-sensitive language
4. **Ask Questions**: Drive comments
5. **Encourage Sharing**: Explicit CTAs
6. **Be Specific**: Concrete details over vague statements
7. **Use Numbers**: Lists and statistics stand out
8. **Tell Stories**: Relatable narratives
9. **Include Emojis**: Visual breaks and emotion
10. **End with CTA**: Clear next step
